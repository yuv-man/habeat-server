# MACRONUTRIENTS × MOOD

## protein
`4kcal/g` | satiety_rank: 1/3 (highest)
neurotransmitter_precursors:
  tryptophan → serotonin (mood stability, sleep, appetite regulation)
  tyrosine → dopamine + norepinephrine (motivation, reward, focus)
  phenylalanine → norepinephrine (alertness, stress response)
deficiency_signals: [low_satiety, mood_instability, sugar_cravings, fatigue, brain_fog, irritability]
ee_link: low_protein_diet → +40% unplanned_snack_frequency | ↑ emotional_eating_risk
optimal_intake: `1.2–2.0g/kg/bodyweight/day` | spread_evenly (`25–40g/meal` > single large dose)
satiety_duration: `3–5h` (vs carbs: `1.5–2.5h`)
mood_impact_onset: `45–90min` post-ingestion (tryptophan crosses BBB with carb co-ingestion)

## carbohydrates
`4kcal/g` | satiety_rank: 3/3 (lowest alone)
mechanism:
  insulin spike → clears competing amino acids → tryptophan:BBB ratio ↑ → serotonin ↑
  simple_carbs: rapid glucose spike → crash → cortisol↑ → cravings → ee_risk ↑
  complex_carbs: slow release → stable glucose → stable mood → ee_risk ↓
blood_glucose_variability: +1 SD variability → 23% ↑ negative_affect, 19% ↑ irritability
ee_link: glucose_dip_below_70mg/dL → prefrontal_cortex_impaired → impulse_control ↓
optimal: low-GI sources (oats, legumes, root_veg, whole_grains) | `25–45g/meal`
avoid: refined_sugar + refined_flour on empty_stomach (peak glucose → crash cycle)

## fats
`9kcal/g` | satiety_rank: 2/3
omega_3 (EPA/DHA):
  mechanism: ↓ neuroinflammation | ↑ serotonin_receptor_sensitivity | ↑ BDNF
  deficiency: ↑ depression_risk (OR 1.4), ↑ impulsive_eating, ↓ mood_regulation
  optimal: `1–3g EPA+DHA/day` | food_sources: [salmon, mackerel, sardines, walnuts, flaxseed]
  ee_link: omega3_deficient → emotional_reactivity ↑ → trigger_sensitivity ↑
omega_6_excess: omega6:omega3 ratio >10:1 → pro-inflammatory → ↑ depressive_symptoms
saturated_fat_excess: ↓ BDNF expression → ↓ mood_plasticity
healthy_fats: avocado, olive_oil, nuts → slow_gastric_emptying → satiety ↑, cravings ↓

## micronutrients_critical_for_ee
magnesium:
  role: regulates HPA_axis (stress_response), 300+ enzymatic_reactions
  deficiency: ↑ cortisol, ↑ anxiety, ↑ cravings (esp. chocolate), impaired_sleep
  food_sources: [dark_chocolate_70%+, leafy_greens, pumpkin_seeds, almonds, black_beans]
vitamin_D:
  role: receptor in every_brain_cell | modulates serotonin_synthesis
  deficiency (< `30ng/mL`): ↑ depression_risk 2×, ↑ binge_eating_episodes
  note: deficiency_common in indoor_workers and northern_latitudes
B_vitamins:
  B6: cofactor in serotonin + dopamine synthesis
  B12 + folate: SAMe production → methylation → neurotransmitter_balance
  deficiency: fatigue, brain_fog, ↑ emotional_reactivity
zinc:
  role: amygdala_function (emotional_processing), insulin_regulation
  deficiency: ↑ anxiety, ↑ impulsive_food_choices, taste_alterations → ↑ sugar_seeking
