# COGNITIVE DISTORTIONS — FOOD & EATING

## overview
cognitive_distortions: automatic_thoughts that are systematically_biased away from reality
in_EE context: distortions → permission_to_eat, hopelessness, shame_amplification
key_principle: thought is not_fact — identify → examine_evidence → reframe

## distortions_most_common_in_EE

### all-or-nothing thinking
pattern: "I ate one cookie → I've ruined the day/week → might as well eat everything"
alias: black-and-white, perfectionism
mechanism: single_deviation → category_shift ("healthy" → "failed") → permission_granting
reframe: "One cookie is one cookie. The next choice is independent."
clinical_note: strongest predictor of restriction-binge_cycle
prevalence: present in ~73% of BED_cases

### catastrophizing
pattern: "I have no willpower" | "I'll never change" | "This always happens to me"
mechanism: single_event generalized to permanent_global_trait
consequence: learned_helplessness → ↓ change_attempt, ↑ EE as coping
reframe: "I ate emotionally tonight. That's one episode, not my identity."

### emotional_reasoning
pattern: "I feel guilty → I must have done something terrible" | "I feel fat → I am fat"
mechanism: emotion as_evidence_of_fact (confuses feeling with reality)
EE_manifestation: feeling_out_of_control → believing_you_are_out_of_control → acting accordingly
reframe: "My emotion is real. It's information, not fact."

### mind_reading (social eating context)
pattern: "Everyone is judging what I'm eating" | "They think I'm greedy"
mechanism: assuming_negative_thoughts of others without_evidence
consequence: ↑ anxiety in social_eating → avoidance OR overeat to appear_normal
reframe: "Others are mostly focused on their own experience."

### should_statements
pattern: "I should be able to control this" | "I shouldn't eat after 8pm" | "I should want salad"
mechanism: rigid_rules → guilt_when_broken → shame_cycle
note: "should" = rule_without_flexibility → invites_violation
reframe: replace_with "I'd prefer to" or "It would be helpful to"

### labeling
pattern: "I'm a stress eater" | "I'm addicted to sugar" | "I'm lazy"
mechanism: single_behavior → fixed_identity (most_damaging distortion for_change)
consequence: identity-consistent_behavior reinforced ("of course I eat when stressed, that's who I am")
reframe: "I ate when stressed today. I'm someone learning to respond differently."

### magnification / minimization
magnify: making mistakes catastrophic | minimizing progress ("anyone could have done that")
in_EE: magnify_slip → minimize_successes → ↓ self_efficacy → ↑ EE
reframe: apply_consistent_scale to both setbacks and progress

### fortune_telling
pattern: "I know I'll eat badly at the party" | "I'll never be able to resist"
mechanism: predicting_negative outcome as if_fact → self-fulfilling_prophecy
reframe: "I don't know what will happen. I can prepare strategies."

### personalization
pattern: "My family's stress is because I'm not eating right" | "My bad mood is ruining everyone's day"
EE_manifestation: taking responsibility for others' emotions → ↑ guilt → EE to self_soothe
reframe: "Other people's emotions have many causes. I'm not responsible for all of them."
